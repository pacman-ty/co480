Approach:
1. First found the longest repeated substring in the string 

2. Then found the indices of where these substrings were in the string 

3. found the difference of these indices and got the GCD of the difference

4. that difference was the keyword length

5. then got 6 substrings based of this length and did a frequency analysis to determine the letter 

6. then used the keyword to decode the message
